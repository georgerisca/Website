Paint & Restoration:

This section is in regard to everything sanding, painting, and general restoration. The first pictures are from when I repainted my car. The reason for repainting was that the car had several damaged areas and I wanted to cover up some of the bondo spots. It was repainted the original color: Estoril Blue. The second set of pictures are of vinyl wrapping. This adds a layer of protection between the paint and elements. The final 2 photos are from a honda CB motorcycle. The outer rims and hubs were sandblasted and then painted black. To finish off, I respoked the wheels and changed the brake shoes.
